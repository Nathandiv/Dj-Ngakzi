Bio

DJ Ngakz, a trailblazing 14-year-old DJ and entrepreneur, is redefining South Africa’s youth culture. This June, in celebration of Youth Month, she will release her highly anticipated single alongside the launch of her new cosmetic range, marking a major milestone in her journey as a rising force in entertainment and business.

With a passion for music and style, DJ Ngakz is more than a DJ—she’s a brand in the making. Her upcoming single embodies the vibrant spirit of South African youth, inspiring her peers to embrace their passions and pursue their dreams fearlessly.
